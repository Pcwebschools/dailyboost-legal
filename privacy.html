Privacy Policy

DailyBoost respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the DailyBoost web application.

  • Basic account information (e.g. email address) if applicable

We do not collect platform passwords or private credentials.

2. How We Use Information

3. Data Sharing

DailyBoost does not sell or rent personal data. Data is shared with third-party platforms only when required to perform actions requested by the user.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect user data. However, no online service can guarantee complete security.

5. User Control

Users may disconnect connected platforms or stop using DailyBoost at any time.
